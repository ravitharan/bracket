Usage of Excel sheet for scheduling and scoring are as follows,

1) Register everyone's attendance in the sheet "attendance" as '1'. If any
player does not turn up for the tournament, mark their attendance as '-1'.

2) Once enough teams have arrived, which is at least 16 men's teams and 4 Boys
and Girls teams, run python script. Python script fill sheets "quali_schedule"
and "quali_score".

3) Share "quali_schedule" sheet to all teams so that they know their turns.

4) Every match result needs to be entered in the sheet "quali_score".

Python script schedule the match as follows,
1) First two rounds are for the attendance registered teams.

2) Any two matches for a team has at least one match gap.

3) All the Boys & Girls matches are on Court 4.

4) Court 5 will be used only once for a team.

5) Matches are chosen randomly.

6) There won't be any schedule for absent teams.
